***** RECIPES ******
1. Chole Kulcha:
     Prepare Chole Ki Subji, make dough for Kulcha, Prepare kulcha dough mixed with stuffings.
     Add Kulcha Dough to Furnance, Takeout the Kulcha from furnance after 5 mins. 
     Serve the Dish with Salads.
2. Strawberry MilkShake:
     Add some Milk to a Mixer. Add Strawberry cutted into pieces into it. Add some Sugar. Mix untill 
     very small pieces of strawberry left. Add some more Suguar if required and mix again.
     Pour the shake in a glass. Serve with Strawberry on the Side,
